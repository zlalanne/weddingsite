Our Story

Nicole and Zack grew up together in Richardson, TX. Even though they "met" in 2002 it took awhile before they finally got engaged.

After they both graduated with their bachelors degrees Zack moved back to Dallas, TX to start a new job as a Software Engineer at Texas Instruments. During this time Nicole continued work on her masters. Once Nicole finished up her Masters in Education, Zack finally decided to pop the question.

Days after getting engaged the couple packed up and headed to Uganda to work with a local orphanage and with the Batwa Pygmies. They had a great time together and once they got back home started to plan the wedding!
