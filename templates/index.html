Our Story

Nicole and Zack grew up together in Richardson, Texas. They first met in 2002 as seventh graders at Richardson West Junior High. However, they didn't become good friends until they continued onto Richardson High School. It was there, in tenth grade, where they began to bond over their shared love of math in Algebra II. Their bond grew and they later started dating. Nicole and Zack became "official" on February 22nd, 2007.

A couple of years later, Nicole's mom shared a memory of Nicole and Zack's high school years with them. Her mom remembered Nicole and Zack studying for their physics class at the house and overhearing Nicole and Zack talk and laugh together. Nicole's mom thought to herself how strange it was that Nicole and Zack already seemed to "fit like a pair of old shoes."

Upon graduation, Nicole and Zack then went off in different directions to their own universities. Zack began at the University of Texas at Arlington and Nicole at Austin College in Sherman. Although they knew it would be tough, Zack and Nicole decided to continue their relationship to see where it went.

In 2009, Zack transferred to University of Texas at Austin while Nicole continued at Austin College. While the distance between them was large, Zack and Nicole visited each other as much as possible to see and support each other. The following year, Nicole studied abroad in Berlin, Germany for a semester. This would be the true test of their relationship. Although the distance was tough, Nicole and Zack's commitment to their relationship remained strong.

Finally the distance began to shrink. Zack completed his bachelors degree in Computer Engineering and returned to Dallas to work as a software engineer at Texas Instruments while Nicole completed her masters degree in education at Austin College. Nicole then returned to Dallas to work as an algebra teacher and junior varsity soccer coach at Richardson High School.

Just days before Zack and Nicole went on a three week trip to Uganda, Zack finally decided to pop the question (6 years later) with a picnic of Nicole's favorite foods and champagne at a park Nicole and Zack used to sneak away to in high school. To celebrate, Nicole and Zack's families met at Nicole's parents' house to share the news with their extended families and to drink to their engagement.

Nicole and Zack are grateful for all of the support and love their families and friends have given to them individually and to their relationship over the years. They now look forward to finally beginning the next chapter of their lives together and can't wait to see what is to come.
