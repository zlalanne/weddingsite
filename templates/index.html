Our Story

Nicole and Zack grew up together in Richardson, TX. Even though they "met" in 2002 it took awhile before they finally got engaged.

After they both graduated with their bachelors degrees Zack moved back to Dallas, TX to start a new job as a Software Engineer at Texas Instruments. During this time Nicole continued work on her masters. Once Nicole finished up her Masters in Education, Zack finally decided to pop the question.

Days after getting engaged the couple packed up and headed to Uganda to work with a local orphanage and with the Batwa Pygmies. They had a great time together and once they got back home started to plan the wedding!

Wedding Party

  • Jackie Streetman

    Matron of Honor

    Jackie is Nicole's older sister and matron of honor. Jackie and Nicole have been through thick and thin together, from plane rides to Midland every other weekend in their childhood to arguing over borrowed clothing in their teenage years (and still somewhat today...). Years later, Jackie moved to Gunter with her husband Corey and two dogs Maggie and Riley. Jackie and Corey then gave us our newest addition, Brennen, almost two years ago. Today, Jackie and Nicole spend most of their time together gabbing and eating good food. Nicole admires Jackie's outgoing and charismatic personality that draws in all people within a ten foot radius.

    Jackie now has the time-consuming, but rewarding job of not only caring for her own family's needs, but also a family in Frisco, for which she nannies.

  • Jacob Lalanne

    Best Man

    Jake is Zack's younger brother and best man. Zack tries to look out for his younger brother and even got Jake his first job and his first car, but both ended badly. Although he lives in Denver, whenever they are together they make the most of the time they have, which usually includes playing video games and drinking beer. Zack admires Jake's work ethic and has accepted the fact that he never will be able to cook a steak or bake a cheesecake as well as he can.

    Jake is currently a chef at a cajun restaurant in downtown Denver.

  • Kaitlin Elledge

    Maid of Honor

    Kaitlin is one of Nicole's college friends. Kaitlin and Nicole played soccer together at Austin College for three years where Nicole's revered Kaitlin's amazing "Camo Keeper" skills. When Nicole and Kaitlin weren't too busy studying, they bonded over a some well-known college games, like card and "pong" games. While Nicole was studying abroad in Germany, Kaitlin visited her and they painted their faces to watch the U.S. women's soccer team play Sweden in the Women's World Cup 2011. Kaitlin and Nicole both moved to Dallas for work and now spend most of their time together at low-key restaurants and bars in Addison and Lower Greenville.

    Kaitlin now works in Addison as an account executive for MetLife and gets the opportunity to name many rental cars as she travels all over Texas and Oklahoma for work.

  • Tyler Hanna

    Groomsmen

    Tyler is one of Zack's oldest friends, going all the way back to the second grade. Together they went on many annual Stillwater trips and got sleep deprived at weekend film races. After high school they went off to the University of Texas together and survived being roommates.

    Tyler is now plotting revenge on Zack's triumphant victory as fantasy football champion.

  • Ashleigh Johnson

    Bridesmaid

    Ashleigh is also one of Nicole's college friends and old teammates. They played for the Roos for three years and Ashleigh dominated the defensive third of the field each year. Ashleigh and Nicole's friendship blossomed from their shared weirdness and love for shenanigans, such as: freezing underwear, bathroom mirror messages and sabotaging each other in any way possible while living together. Ashleigh now lives in Austin and Ashleigh and Nicole make the trek to see each other as often as their time and money permits.

    Ashleigh is currently finishing up her masters degree in public health at the University of Texas at Austin and applying to medical schools in Texas.

  • Gib Reynolds

    Groomsmen

    Gib is another old friend of Zack who he has known most of his life. Gib and Zack bonded over their constant listening to Brand New and playing obscure high school sports. Conversations are now mostly filled with talk of good beer and new tech along with the occasional BIG XII trash talk.

    Gib is now a professor at a college that has Bears on campus and will probably be a part of the next big thing.

  • Mackenzie Lund

    Bridesmaid

    Mackenzie is yet another one of Nicole's college friends and teammates. While Mackenzie wore Austin College's crimson and gold colors, she used her dizzying speed on the flank of the soccer field to up her stats in assists and goals. Mackenzie and Nicole also lived together during their college career. Nicole most enjoyed Mackenzie's ability to beautifully interpret songs through dance with Ashleigh.

    Mackenzie is now finishing up her law degree at Alabama. She plans to return to Texas to take the bar, so if you're look for a very capable lawyer, look no further!
